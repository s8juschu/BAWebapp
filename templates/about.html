About SwimPlanner

Project for swimmer

SwimPlanner was designed as part of a bachelor thesis. It's goal is to provide an interface for creating and managing training plans, as well as sharing them. It is possible to share the plans with a group or individual swimmers. You can also export them as a FitFile on a Garmin watch to display as a training plan on the watch.

If you have any questions or remarks, feel free to contact us!
